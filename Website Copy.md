### **2.1 Belt Fitting & Vulcanising**

**SEO Title:** Conveyor Belt Fitting & Vulcanising Services | N E Vulcanising  
**Meta Description:** Precision conveyor belt installation and vulcanising services. We offer hot vulcanising, cold vulcanising, and mechanical joints to minimise downtime.  
**Target Keywords:** hot vulcanising UK, cold vulcanising, conveyor belt fitting, belt splicing.  
The integrity of a conveyor belt splice is the ultimate determinant of overall system longevity. A compromised splice leads to tracking errors, material spillage, and eventual catastrophic tearing. At N E Vulcanising, our engineers utilise state-of-the-art pressing equipment and chemical compounds to perform high-strength splicing that withstands the extreme tensions of heavy industrial material handling. We offer a suite of joining techniques tailored to the specific environmental and operational demands of your facility.  
**Hot Vulcanising:** This represents the pinnacle of belt splicing technology. By applying precise, computer-controlled heat and extreme pressure, we create a seamless splice that bonds the rubber layers on a molecular level. This method is the definitive choice for high-tension, heavy-duty applications.  
**Cold Vulcanising:** For environments where hot presses are impractical, highly flammable, or where rapid turnaround is the primary requirement, our cold vulcanising process utilises premium industrial two-part adhesives to bond the belt layers securely.

### **2.2 Belt Repairs**

**SEO Title:** On-Site Conveyor Belt Repairs & Splicing | N E Vulcanising  
**Meta Description:** Rapid, root-cause conveyor belt repairs across the UK. We fix longitudinal rips, edge wear, and impact damage to get your system running efficiently.  
**Target Keywords:** conveyor belt repairs UK, on-site conveyor repair, fix torn conveyor belt.  
A damaged belt does not always necessitate a highly expensive full replacement. Our diagnostic engineering approach ensures that we identify the root cause of the damage prior to executing any repair. Whether the damage stems from severe tracking misalignment, seized idler rollers creating friction heat, or sharp material carryback becoming trapped between the belt and the tail pulley, we address the underlying mechanical failure. We expertly repair longitudinal rips, heavy edge wear, and impact puncture damage using advanced rubber repair materials and localised vulcanising techniques that restore the belt's structural integrity.  
Our engineers arrive at your facility fully self-supported. Our mobile fleet is equipped with portable presses, generators, high-capacity compressors, specialised pneumatic tooling, and all necessary safety equipment to execute repairs directly at your facility, minimising the costly and time-consuming process of dismantling conveyor infrastructure.

### **2.3 Drum Lagging**

**SEO Title:** Conveyor Drum Lagging Services | Rubber & Ceramic | N E Vulcanising  
**Meta Description:** Prevent conveyor belt slippage with our professional drum lagging services. We offer plain rubber, diamond groove, and ceramic lagging solutions.  
**Target Keywords:** conveyor drum lagging, diamond groove lagging, ceramic pulley lagging.  
Slippage between the conveyor belt and the drive drum causes catastrophic wear to the bottom cover of the belt, generates severe frictional heat, and results in massive energy loss. N E Vulcanising provides expert drum lagging services designed to dramatically increase the coefficient of friction between the pulley and the belt, shed unwanted water and slurry, and prolong the operational life of both the drum shell and the conveyor belt itself.

| Lagging Configuration | Optimal Environmental Application | Primary Engineering Benefits |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Plain Smooth Rubber** | Dry, general-purpose material handling and non-drive deflection pulleys. | Provides standard friction enhancement while protecting the raw steel drum shell from abrasive wear and corrosion. |
| **Diamond Groove Rubber** | Wet, muddy, or highly contaminated environments (e.g., washing plants, agriculture). | The geometric grooves actively channel water and debris away from the contact surface. |
| **Ceramic Embedded** | High-tension, highly abrasive, or extremely wet systems (e.g., hard-rock mining). | Delivers unparalleled grip and wear resistance. Utilises high-alumina ceramic tiles embedded within a rubber matrix. |

Our roller inventory encompasses a complete selection of universal return rollers, heavy-duty troughing idler sets, twin configurations, and top set rollers designed to guide belts accurately and minimise frictional wear.(Links to: /contact/\#enquiry-form)

*   **Can repairs be carried out on-site?**  
    Yes, our experienced team can carry out most conveyor belt repairs directly at your site, ensuring minimal disruption and a fast turnaround. We offer hot and cold vulcanising, as well as mechanical and clip joints.

Contact us to book a site survey for expert advice.

*   **Which industries do you serve?**  
    We serve a diverse range of industries, including construction, demolition, quarrying, mining, road construction, recycling and waste management, washing systems, agriculture, and warehouse logistics.
